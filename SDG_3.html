About SDG-3

SDG-3: Good Health and Well-being aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all ages.

Key Objectives

  • Reduce maternal mortality and preventable deaths of newborns and children.
  • End the epidemics of AIDS, tuberculosis, malaria, and other communicable diseases.
  • Reduce mortality from non-communicable diseases and promote mental health and well-being.
  • Strengthen the prevention and treatment of substance abuse, including narcotic drug abuse and harmful use of alcohol.

Strategies

To achieve SDG-3, efforts are needed to improve access to quality healthcare services, strengthen health systems, promote health education and awareness, and address social determinants of health.

Challenges

Despite progress, challenges such as inadequate healthcare infrastructure, unequal access to healthcare services, and emerging infectious diseases continue to affect health outcomes globally.

Impact of COVID-19

The COVID-19 pandemic has highlighted the importance of strong health systems, global cooperation, and investment in pandemic preparedness and response.
