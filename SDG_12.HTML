About SDG-12

SDG-12: Responsible Consumption and Production aims to ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns by promoting resource efficiency, sustainable infrastructure, and responsible consumption.

Key Objectives

  • Implement the 10-Year Framework of Programmes on Sustainable Consumption and Production (10YFP) to achieve sustainable consumption and production patterns.
  • By 2030, achieve sustainable management and efficient use of natural resources.
  • By 2030, halve per capita global food waste at the retail and consumer levels and reduce food losses along production and supply chains, including post-harvest losses.
  • Encourage companies, especially large and transnational companies, to adopt sustainable practices and integrate sustainability information into their reporting cycles.

Strategies

To achieve SDG-12, efforts are needed to promote sustainable lifestyles, improve resource efficiency in production and consumption, reduce waste generation, promote sustainable public procurement practices, and enhance global cooperation and partnerships for sustainable development.

Challenges

Despite progress, challenges such as overconsumption, unsustainable production practices, waste generation, and inadequate infrastructure for sustainable consumption and production continue to pose significant obstacles to achieving SDG-12.

Impact of COVID-19

The COVID-19 pandemic has highlighted the need for more resilient and sustainable supply chains, as well as the importance of responsible consumption and production practices to ensure the availability of essential goods and services in times of crisis.
