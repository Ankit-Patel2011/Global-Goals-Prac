About SDG-1

SDG-1: No Poverty aims to end poverty in all its forms everywhere. It seeks to ensure social protection for the poor and vulnerable, increase access to basic services, and support people affected by crises.

Key Objectives

  • End extreme poverty by 2030.
  • Implement social protection systems for all.
  • Ensure equal rights and access to economic resources.

Strategies

To achieve SDG-1, efforts are needed to promote sustainable economic growth, create jobs, provide access to basic services such as education and healthcare, and implement social protection systems.

Challenges

Despite progress, challenges such as income inequality, lack of access to education and healthcare, and discrimination continue to hinder efforts to eradicate poverty.

Impact of COVID-19

The COVID-19 pandemic has exacerbated poverty and inequality, highlighting the need for urgent action to support the most vulnerable populations.
