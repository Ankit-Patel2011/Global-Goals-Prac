About SDG-16

SDG-16: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions aims to promote peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development, provide access to justice for all, and build effective, accountable, and inclusive institutions at all levels.

Key Objectives

  • Promote peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development, provide access to justice for all, and build effective, accountable, and inclusive institutions at all levels.
  • By 2030, significantly reduce all forms of violence and related death rates everywhere.
  • Ensure equal access to justice for all and build effective, accountable, and inclusive institutions at all levels.
  • Develop effective, accountable, and transparent institutions at all levels.

Strategies

To achieve SDG-16, efforts are needed to strengthen the rule of law, reduce violence, combat corruption and bribery, ensure access to justice for all, promote responsive, inclusive, participatory, and representative decision-making, and strengthen institutions for sustainable development.

Challenges

Despite progress, challenges such as conflict, violence, corruption, impunity, lack of access to justice, and weak institutions continue to undermine peace, stability, and sustainable development in many parts of the world.

Impact of COVID-19

The COVID-19 pandemic has exacerbated existing inequalities and vulnerabilities, increased social unrest and violence, disrupted justice systems, and highlighted the importance of strong and inclusive institutions for effective crisis response, recovery, and sustainable development.
