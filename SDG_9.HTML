About SDG-9

SDG-9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure aims to build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation.

Key Objectives

  • Develop quality, reliable, sustainable, and resilient infrastructure, including transportation, energy, and information and communication technology.
  • Promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization and increase the share of industry in employment and gross domestic product (GDP).
  • Foster innovation and enhance technological capabilities to support sustainable development.
  • Facilitate sustainable and resilient infrastructure development in developing countries through enhanced financial, technological, and technical support.

Strategies

To achieve SDG-9, efforts are needed to invest in infrastructure development, promote sustainable industrial practices, support small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs), foster research and development, and strengthen international cooperation and partnerships.

Challenges

Despite progress, challenges such as inadequate infrastructure, limited access to technology and innovation, and environmental degradation continue to hinder efforts to achieve sustainable industrialization and infrastructure development.

Impact of COVID-19

The COVID-19 pandemic has disrupted global supply chains, highlighted the importance of digital infrastructure, and underscored the need for resilient and sustainable infrastructure systems to support economic recovery and growth.
