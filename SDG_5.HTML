About SDG-5

SDG-5: Gender Equality aims to achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls.

Key Objectives

  • End all forms of discrimination and violence against women and girls.
  • Eliminate harmful practices such as child marriage and female genital mutilation.
  • Ensure equal opportunities for leadership and participation in decision-making at all levels.
  • Ensure universal access to sexual and reproductive health and rights.

Strategies

To achieve SDG-5, efforts are needed to enact and enforce laws and policies that promote gender equality, eliminate gender-based violence, provide equal access to education and employment, and challenge harmful gender norms and stereotypes.

Challenges

Despite progress, challenges such as gender-based discrimination, limited access to education and healthcare, unequal representation in decision-making, and persistent gender stereotypes continue to hinder efforts to achieve gender equality.

Impact of COVID-19

The COVID-19 pandemic has exacerbated gender inequalities, with women and girls facing increased risks of domestic violence, economic insecurity, and barriers to accessing essential services.
