About SDG-17

SDG-17: Partnerships for the Goals aims to strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development.

Key Objectives

  • Strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development.
  • Enhance the global partnership for sustainable development, complemented by multi-stakeholder partnerships that mobilize and share knowledge, expertise, technology, and financial resources to support the achievement of the sustainable development goals in all countries.
  • Promote the development, transfer, dissemination, and diffusion of environmentally sound technologies to developing countries on favorable terms, including on concessional and preferential terms, as mutually agreed.
  • Enhance international support for implementing effective and targeted capacity-building in developing countries to support national plans to implement all the sustainable development goals, including through North-South, South-South, and triangular cooperation.

Strategies

To achieve SDG-17, efforts are needed to enhance global partnerships, mobilize resources, promote technology transfer, build capacity, promote trade, and strengthen cooperation among governments, civil society, the private sector, and international organizations.

Challenges

Despite progress, challenges such as limited financial resources, inadequate technology transfer, insufficient capacity-building, trade barriers, and lack of effective cooperation continue to hinder the achievement of the sustainable development goals.

Impact of COVID-19

The COVID-19 pandemic has underscored the importance of international cooperation, solidarity, and partnerships for addressing global challenges, promoting resilience, and accelerating progress towards the sustainable development goals.
