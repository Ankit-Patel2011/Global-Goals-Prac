About SDG-6

SDG-6: Clean Water and Sanitation aims to ensure access to clean water and sanitation for all.

Key Objectives

  • Ensure universal and equitable access to safe and affordable drinking water for all.
  • End open defecation and provide access to adequate and equitable sanitation and hygiene for all.
  • Improve water quality by reducing pollution, eliminating dumping, and minimizing the release of hazardous chemicals and materials.
  • Substantially increase water-use efficiency and ensure sustainable withdrawals and supply of freshwater to address water scarcity.

Strategies

To achieve SDG-6, efforts are needed to invest in water and sanitation infrastructure, promote hygiene education and behavior change, protect and restore water-related ecosystems, and strengthen water governance and management.

Challenges

Despite progress, challenges such as inadequate infrastructure, water scarcity, pollution, and unequal access to water and sanitation services persist, particularly in rural and marginalized communities.

Impact of COVID-19

The COVID-19 pandemic has underscored the importance of access to clean water and sanitation for preventing the spread of infectious diseases and protecting public health.
